The moon illuminated the sparse field of newgrass, and the sky connected with the river.Ihope that in a desperate situation, love can pass the test without any danger

月冷疏原，天垂野流
祝今晚，爱意绝渡逢舟



Love is a game of voluntary loss. It can drive strategists to surrender on their own, and it can also drive bad people to surrender their guns on their own

爱本就是甘拜下风的一局
可令谋者自降，恶党缴枪



The moon is a large tattoo on the body at night,they kiss and tangle in silence, and die together at dawn

月亮是烙入漆夜的刺青，
谧静间拥吻，消亡天明。



Great poet, you haven't had many failed works in your life, only I am the abandoned manuscript waiting to be destroyed

大诗人，你这一生败笔寥寥
偏我是待焚的废稿



Wishing us the courage to become the best interpreters of each other's lives

祝我们勇敢，做彼此的独白



Unfortunately, I missed your past. Why don't you let me accompany you towards a better future

长虹是雨迟来的信者
错过命定的潮湿，姗姗而至。



Ordinary and uninteresting,I met someone who amazed me

寡言的时代里，
我是千场黑白哑剧，你是乍起峥然音。
